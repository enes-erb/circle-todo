Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is my data stored in the cloud?

A: No, all your data is stored locally on your device. We prioritize your privacy - your tasks never leave your phone.

Q: How do I backup my tasks?

A: Currently, tasks are backed up through iOS's standard iCloud backup. We're working on manual export features for future updates.

Q: Can I sync between devices?

A: Circle Todo is designed as a privacy-first, local-only app. Cloud sync is not available to ensure your data remains private.

Q: How do I report a bug?

A: Please email us at muhammeteneserb@gmail.com with a description of the issue and your iOS version.

Q: Is Circle Todo free?

A: Yes, Circle Todo is completely free with no hidden costs or in-app purchases.

Privacy Policy

Circle Todo is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. All information remains on your device. For more details, see our full Privacy Policy.
